About Devkant Foundation

Founded with a vision to create equal opportunities and empower communities through a wide range of social initiatives.

Our Mission

At Devkant Foundation , we believe that providing essential services in education, health, and community development is a fundamental right. We are dedicated to empowering individuals and communities through comprehensive programs focused on creating a positive social impact. Based in Bihar, India, we work tirelessly to ensure that everyone has the opportunity to thrive and build a better future.

Our comprehensive programs focus on computer education, health and medical services, rural & community development, IT consultancy services, women empowerment, and disability & elderly care, which prepare individuals for meaningful employment and entrepreneurship opportunities.

Registered NGO committed to social welfare and education

Our Services

Dedicated to uplifting lives through holistic programs that promote equality, dignity, and opportunity.

Computer Education

Basic to advanced computer literacy programs covering essential software, internet usage, and digital communication skills for students and adults.

  • • Microsoft Office Suite
  • • Internet & Email
  • • Digital Communication
  • • Basic Troubleshooting

Health & Medical Services

NGOs provide free healthcare, medical camps, and awareness programs, ensuring access to essential services and promoting healthier communities.

  • • Free medical camps and health check-ups
  • • Mental health counseling
  • • HIV/AIDS, TB, cancer awareness
  • • Blood donation drives

Rural & Community Development

NGOs focus on empowering rural communities by creating opportunities for sustainable growth, improving living standards, and fostering social inclusion.

  • • Rural livelihood programs
  • • Access to clean water & sanitation projects
  • • Housing support for poor families
  • • Agricultural training and farmer support

IT Consultancy Services

We provide professional IT consultancy to help organizations optimize technology use, improve efficiency, and achieve digital growth.

  • • IT strategy & digital transformation
  • • System integration & software solutions
  • • Cybersecurity & data protection
  • • Cloud migration & services

Women Empowerment

Focused on uplifting women through education, skill training, and awareness, enabling them to achieve independence, equality, and leadership in society.

  • • SHGs & microfinance support
  • • Skill development & employment training
  • • Health & nutrition programs for women
  • • Awareness on gender equality

Disability & Elderly Care

Support services include therapy, skill training, and accessibility aid for persons with disabilities, along with shelter, healthcare, and community programs for the elderly.

  • • Special Education Support
  • • Old-age homes and senior citizen support
  • • Helplines for elderly abuse
  • • Community engagement activities
